Capabilities & Services

From programmatic audio synthesis to privacy-first applications, our R&D studio delivers technology that respects users and rewards attention. Explore our full service matrix below.

01 · Audio Synthesis

Programmatic audio that thinks for itself

We develop real-time DSP engines, generative sound design tools, and procedural audio systems that produce infinite, organic variations. Perfect for apps requiring ambient soundscapes, adaptive audio feedback, and creative sound design.

  • Real-time synthesis with low-latency signal processing pipelines
  • Procedural audio for games, wellness, meditation and ambient applications
  • Custom oscillators, filters, and modulation architectures
  • Adaptive audio that responds to biometric and motion sensors
  • High-performance native DSP on iOS, macOS and Android
02 · Visual Interaction

Immersive motion & interaction systems

From micro-interactions to cinematic transitions, we engineer motion systems that feel inevitable. Every animation has purpose, every gesture has a response, and every interface feels alive without being distracting.

  • Procedural UI animation systems driven by physics and easing curves
  • Haptic feedback design integrated with visual transitions
  • Generative particle systems, shaders and procedural backgrounds
  • Realtime rendering with Metal, Vulkan and WebGL/WebGPU
  • Motion choreography aligned with brand identity
03 · Privacy Engineering

Privacy is architecture, not a feature

We design systems where user data stays on the device by default. Our privacy engineering practice covers zero-knowledge encryption, secure enclaves, on-device ML inference, and complete transparency in data flows.

  • End-to-end encryption with locally-managed keys (no server escrow)
  • On-device machine learning using Core ML, TensorFlow Lite and ExecuTorch
  • Secure Enclave and Android Keystore integration
  • GDPR, CCPA, PIPL and LGPD compliant data architecture
  • Privacy manifests and App Privacy Report alignment
04 · Procedural Generation

Infinite variety, zero repetition

We break free from static content by designing procedural systems that generate unique, organic experiences on every interaction. From 3D geometry to sound textures, our engines produce endlessly varied output.

  • Procedural geometry: meshes, terrain, generative architecture
  • Procedural textures and materials using GLSL / Metal Shading Language
  • Generative audio, music and ambient compositions
  • Seeded randomness for reproducible creative output
  • Real-time procedural worlds and environments
