Frequently Asked

Quick answers

Common questions about working with us.

What types of projects do you take on?

We focus on privacy-first applications for iOS, iPadOS, macOS, watchOS, and Android. Projects typically range from native app development to full R&D partnerships in programmatic audio, visual interaction, and privacy engineering.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity. A focused single-feature app typically takes 3-6 months. Full product development ranges from 6-18 months. We work in agile sprints with weekly deliverables.

Do you offer ongoing support?

Yes — we offer comprehensive post-launch support including bug fixes, OS updates, ASO optimization, and feature iteration. Many of our clients retain us on a long-term basis.

What's your privacy stance?

Privacy is foundational. Every product we build follows local-first principles: data stays on the device by default, with end-to-end encryption when sync is required. We never sell or share user data.
