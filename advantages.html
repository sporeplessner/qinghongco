Our Approach

Complex tech, simple interface

The hardest problems in software aren't technical — they're about translating sophisticated capabilities into experiences that feel intuitive. Our strength is bridging that gap.

We don't show off complexity. We hide it. Every advanced capability we build — be it procedural generation, on-device ML, or zero-knowledge encryption — exists to make the user experience simpler, not more impressive.

This principle guides every choice: technology serves experience, not the other way around.

Studio Journey

From research lab to global studio

Selected milestones in our ongoing exploration of technology, design, and privacy.

2018 · Founded

Studio Founded in Bristol

Qinghongco established at Bristol & Bath Science Park with a focus on programmatic audio and visual interaction.

2019 · First App

First App Store Launch

Released our debut local-first productivity app, establishing the data-sovereignty pattern that defines our work.

2020 · Privacy Framework

Zero-Cloud Architecture

Pioneered our privacy engineering framework — local-first by default, end-to-end encrypted, zero server escrow.

2022 · Product Matrix

App Portfolio Expansion

Shipped our full product matrix: workflow, wellness, security, creativity, and finance — each locally processed.
